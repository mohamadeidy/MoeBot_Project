B&M bot / MoeBot Price Action Strategy — Current v1.05

Core principle:
ZONE -> REACTION -> CONFIRMATION

The bot does not start from BUY/SELL indicators. It starts from chart location:
1. Where is price?
2. Is price at support, resistance, range edge, trend pullback zone, sweep level, breakout level, or mid-range?
3. What reaction happened at that location?
4. Was there confirmation?
5. Is there room to target?
6. Where is invalidation?

Decision order:
Location first.
Direction second.
Confirmation third.
Trade last.

============================================================
TIMEFRAMES
============================================================

H4:
- Major context zones.
- Major support/resistance.
- Adds strength/confluence.
- Not a hard gate.

H1:
- Main support/resistance map.
- Main range highs/lows.
- Main target/risk context.

M15:
- Execution.
- Reaction.
- Confirmation.
- Local zones if no H1/H4 location is currently active.

No valid location = no trade.

============================================================
MAIN SETUPS
============================================================

1) Resistance Rejection Sell
- Price reaches resistance/range high.
- Breakout fails or price rejects the zone.
- Wick rejection or failed push-through appears.
- Confirmation: micro low break / close below rejection structure.
- BUY is blocked at resistance unless breakout is confirmed.
- SELL is allowed after confirmation.

2) Support Rejection Buy
- Price reaches support/range low.
- Breakdown fails or price rejects the zone.
- Wick rejection or failed breakdown appears.
- Confirmation: micro high break / close above rejection structure.
- SELL is blocked at support unless breakdown is confirmed.
- BUY is allowed after confirmation.

3) Trend Pullback Continuation
- Trend structure exists.
- Price pulls back to value/structure.
- Structure remains valid.
- Rejection appears.
- Micro structure breaks in trend direction.
- Trade continuation.

4) Real Breakout / Breakdown
- Price closes beyond a zone.
- Strong body.
- Opposite wick is small.
- Wick-only breakout is invalid.
- Entry requires hold/retest/micro continuation confirmation.

5) Liquidity Sweep Reclaim
- Price sweeps a swing or range edge.
- Price reclaims back inside.
- Micro break confirms reclaim direction.

============================================================
GUARDS
============================================================

Buy-into-resistance guard:
- BUY is blocked at or near strong resistance unless breakout is confirmed.

Sell-into-support guard:
- SELL is blocked at or near strong support unless breakdown is confirmed.

Anti-wick breakout:
- Wick alone is not enough.
- Need close beyond the zone.

Anti-chase:
- Do not enter too far from confirmation trigger.

Room-to-next-H1-level:
- BUY needs room before next H1 resistance.
- SELL needs room before next H1 support.

OneOpenPositionPerSymbol:
- Only one open EA position per symbol.

============================================================
POSITION MANAGEMENT v1.05
============================================================

Every SL layer only proposes a stop.
The bot chooses the best valid SL.

BUY:
- Higher SL is better.
- SL never moves down.

SELL:
- Lower SL is better.
- SL never moves up.

Layers:

1) Money Protection
Purpose: protect meaningful dollar profit before the trade can return to full loss.
Examples:
- XAGUSD: +12 -> protect 2, +20 -> protect 10, +30 -> protect 15
- XAUUSD: +15 -> protect 3, +30 -> protect 12, +50 -> protect 25
- WTI: +10 -> protect 3, +20 -> protect 8, +35 -> protect 15
- US30/US100: +20 -> protect 5, +40 -> protect 15, +70 -> protect 35
- Forex: +5 -> protect 1, +10 -> protect 3, +15 -> protect 7

2) Profit Lock by R
- +0.75R -> protect +0.05R
- +1.00R -> protect +0.30R
- +1.50R -> protect +0.75R

3) Protected Running SL
- After the trade is protected, protect a percentage of open profit.
- Current: 40% of open profit.

4) Structure Trailing
- BUY: trail under micro swing lows if stronger.
- SELL: trail above micro swing highs if stronger.

5) Running TP Extension
- TP extends only if the trade is protected.
- BUY: SL must be above entry.
- SELL: SL must be below entry.

============================================================
NOT ALLOWED
============================================================

- No score system.
- No A/B/C grading.
- No regime brain.
- No EMA/RSI/ADX as entry triggers.
- No martingale.
- No grid.
- No loss averaging.
- No strategic consumed-zone memory.

If price returns to the same zone and creates a valid fresh setup, the bot is allowed to trade it again.

============================================================
MONITORING PRIORITIES
============================================================

1. Does v1.05 protect winners correctly?
2. Does Protected Running SL trail enough?
3. Do winners still return to loss after protection?
4. Do losing trades fail early before full SL?
5. Does USDCAD H4 support continue losing?
6. Do M15 local zones keep underperforming?
7. Do breakout setups fail quickly?
