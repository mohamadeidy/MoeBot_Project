============================================================
2) CORE STRATEGY PRINCIPLE
============================================================

This bot is not an indicator-signal bot.

Final decision model:
ZONE -> REACTION -> CONFIRMATION

Decision order:
1. Location first.
2. Direction second.
3. Confirmation third.
4. Trade last.

The bot must ask:
- Where is price?
- Is it at support, resistance, range edge, pullback zone, sweep level, breakout level, or mid-range?
- What did price do there?
- Did it reject, break, fail, sweep, reclaim, or continue?
- Is there confirmation?
- Is there room to target?
- Where is invalidation?

Not allowed:
- No score system.
- No A/B/C grading.
- No regime brain.
- No EMA/RSI/ADX as entry triggers.
- No martingale.
- No grid.
- No cooldown/max-trades-per-day as strategy logic.
- No strategic consumed-zone blocking.

Important:
If price returns to the same support/resistance and gives a valid fresh setup, the bot is allowed to trade it again.
We decided NOT to block repeated zones.

============================================================
3) TIMEFRAME ROLES
============================================================

H4:
- Major context zones.
- Major support/resistance.
- Adds strength/context.
- Not a hard gate.

H1:
- Main support/resistance map.
- Main battle lines.
- Main zones for range highs/lows.

M15:
- Execution timeframe.
- Reaction.
- Confirmation.
- Entry trigger.
- Local M15 zones only if valid location exists and there is room.

Rule:
The bot does not trade only at H1/H4 levels.
But it never trades without a valid location.

Valid locations:
1. H4/H1 support or resistance zone.
2. M15 local support/resistance or range edge.
3. Trend pullback zone.
4. Liquidity sweep/reclaim level.
5. Real breakout level with confirmation.

No location = no trade.

============================================================
4) MAIN SETUPS
============================================================

1. Resistance Rejection Sell
- Price reaches resistance.
- Breakout fails or price rejects the zone.
- Wick rejection or failed push-through appears.
- Confirmation: micro low break / close below rejection structure.
- BUY is blocked at resistance unless breakout is confirmed.
- SELL is allowed after confirmation.

2. Support Rejection Buy
- Price reaches support.
- Breakdown fails or price rejects the zone.
- Wick rejection or failed breakdown appears.
- Confirmation: micro high break / close above rejection structure.
- SELL is blocked at support unless breakdown is confirmed.
- BUY is allowed after confirmation.

3. Trend Pullback Continuation
- Trend structure exists.
- Price pulls back to value/structure.
- Structure remains valid.
- Rejection appears.
- Micro structure breaks in trend direction.
- Trade continuation.

4. Real Breakout
- Price closes beyond a zone.
- Strong body.
- Opposite wick is small.
- No wick-only breakout.
- Entry needs hold/retest/micro continuation confirmation.

5. Liquidity Sweep Reclaim
- Price sweeps a swing.
- Price reclaims back inside.
- Micro break confirms reclaim direction.
- Trade after confirmation.

============================================================
5) GUARDS
============================================================

Buy-into-resistance guard:
If price is at or near strong resistance without confirmed breakout, BUY is blocked.

Sell-into-support guard:
If price is at or near strong support without confirmed breakdown, SELL is blocked.

Anti-wick breakout:
A wick alone is not a breakout.
A valid breakout needs a close beyond the zone.

Anti-chase:
Do not enter too far from the confirmation trigger.

Room-to-next-H1-level:
Before BUY, there must be enough room before next H1 resistance.
Before SELL, there must be enough room before next H1 support.

One position per symbol:
If a symbol already has an open EA position, new entries on that symbol are blocked with POSITION_ALREADY_OPEN.

============================================================
6) POSITION MANAGEMENT
============================================================

Initial SL:
- Behind invalidation structure.
- Sell from resistance: above rejection high / wick / zone.
- Buy from support: below rejection low / wick / zone.
- Breakout: behind retest / broken zone.
- Pullback: behind pullback swing.

Initial TP:
- Structure target or fallback RR.
- TP can be extended later only if trade is protected.

============================================================
7) SL / PROFIT MANAGEMENT v1.05
============================================================

Current SL logic:
Each layer proposes a stop loss.
The bot chooses the best valid stop only.

For BUY:
- Higher SL is better.
- SL must never move down.

For SELL:
- Lower SL is better.
- SL must never move up.

Layers:

1. Money Protection
Purpose:
Protect meaningful dollar profit early so a trade that was in good profit does not return to full loss.

Threshold examples:
XAGUSD:
- Profit 12 USD -> protect 2 USD
- Profit 20 USD -> protect 10 USD
- Profit 30 USD -> protect 15 USD

XAUUSD:
- Profit 15 USD -> protect 3 USD
- Profit 30 USD -> protect 12 USD
- Profit 50 USD -> protect 25 USD

WTI:
- Profit 10 USD -> protect 3 USD
- Profit 20 USD -> protect 8 USD
- Profit 35 USD -> protect 15 USD

US30 / US100:
- Profit 20 USD -> protect 5 USD
- Profit 40 USD -> protect 15 USD
- Profit 70 USD -> protect 35 USD

Forex:
- Profit 5 USD -> protect 1 USD
- Profit 10 USD -> protect 3 USD
- Profit 15 USD -> protect 7 USD

2. Profit Lock by Risk
- At +0.75R -> protect +0.05R
- At +1.00R -> protect +0.30R
- At +1.50R -> protect +0.75R

3. Protected Running SL
- New v1.05 logic.
- After a trade is protected, it protects a percentage of open profit.
- Current setting: ProtectedRunningSL_KeepOpenProfitPct = 0.40
Example:
If a trade is floating +40 USD, Protected Running SL proposes protection of about 16 USD.

4. Structure Trailing
- BUY: trail below new micro swing lows if better.
- SELL: trail above new micro swing highs if better.

5. Running TP Extension
- TP extension is allowed only if trade is actually protected.
- BUY: SL must be above entry.
- SELL: SL must be below entry.
- If not protected, TP extension is blocked.

Confirmed from logs:
- v1.05 ran successfully.
- Money Protection worked.
- Protected Running SL worked.
- TP_EXTEND_PROTECTED appeared only after protection.
- SL did not move backward.
